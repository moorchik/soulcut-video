FAQ

Common questions about working with us

What’s the typical turnaround?

It depends on the project length and complexity. Highlights usually 1–2 weeks; full films 2–4 weeks. We’ll give you a clear timeline when you send the details.

What file format do you need?

We work with all common formats (ProRes, H.264, etc.) and major NLE project files. We’ll confirm the best delivery option for your workflow.

How do I send you the footage?

We use Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer — whatever you prefer. For large projects we can set up a shared folder and discuss backup options.

Do you do revisions?

Yes. We include one round of revisions in our standard workflow. Additional rounds can be arranged if needed.

What’s your pricing?

Rates depend on project type and length. Send us your project details and we’ll reply with a quote within 24 hours.
